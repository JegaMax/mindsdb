About This Application

Welcome to the Company Knowledge Base application powered by MindsDB. This tool leverages semantic search capabilities to help you find information across various departments and document types within our organization.

Key features:

  • Search: Use natural language to search for company policies, procedures, and information.
  • Browse: View all documents by department or type.
  • Add Documents: Contribute to the knowledge base by adding new information.
  • Summarize: Get AI-generated summaries of lengthy documents.
  • Chat: Interact with an AI assistant for quick answers based on company knowledge.

This application uses MindsDB's Knowledge Bases for semantic search and integrates with AI models for summarization and chat functionalities.
